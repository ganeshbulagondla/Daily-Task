About Soundarya

Soundarya was one of the most famous actresses in South Indian Cinema. She acted mainly in Telugu, Tamil, Kannada, and Hindi films.

Her original name was Sowmya Satyanarayana. She was born on 18 July 1972. She completed her BSc before entering the film industry.

She acted in more than 100+ movies and became one of the most respected actresses because of her natural acting.
